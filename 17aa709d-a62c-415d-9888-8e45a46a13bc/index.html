Sci-Fi Thrillers "Megami, Legacy for the Gods and Megami, Search for the Gods", an H. Scott Hughes Film(s) Written by H. Scott Hughes, Executive Producers H. Scott Hughes and Yvette Lu, with music by H. Scott Hughes and Yvette Lu. Stephen McKinnon is Technical Advisor on Megami: Legacy for the Gods and Associate Producer on Megami: Search for the Gods.
